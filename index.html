Who We Are

What Is The Process

01. Register

Provide information about your business below, and from there we will register and verify its eligibility with the Class Administrator.

02. Advocate

Once registered, we audit, contest and advocate for you to ensure the Class Administrator has accounted for the proper amount of interchange fees you've paid during the term.

03. File

Once we're satisfied the Class Administrator has accounted for all the fees you've paid, we file the Settlement Claim(s) on your behalf for zero up front costs.

04. Pay You

After the Registration date has passed and amounts are approved, we remit the payout to you, minus the LSS fee. We only get paid when you get paid.
