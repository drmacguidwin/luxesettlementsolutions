What is this about?

If your business accepted Visa or Mastercard between 2004 and 2019, you may be entitled to part of a multi-billion dollar class action settlement related to interchange fees.

Who is eligible?

Most businesses that accepted credit card payments from 2004 to 2019 are eligible. You do not need to still be in business to claim.
