What is the Settlement?

In 2005, U.S. Merchants brought a class-action lawsuit against VISA, Mastercard and their bank affiliates for allegedly inflating the interchange fees they charged Merchants for credit card processing. The lawsuit was settled in 2018, but the settlement amount and processes for its distribution were not determined until late 2023. Any Merchant or Business who accepted VISA and/or Mastercard from January 1, 2004 to January 25, 2019 is likely eligible to receive a portion of the settlement amount.
